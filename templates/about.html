What is MindCheck?

MindCheck is a machine learning web application that predicts whether a tech industry worker may benefit from mental health treatment, based on their workplace environment and personal background.

It uses a Random Forest Classifier — an ensemble ML algorithm — trained on real survey data collected from tech professionals worldwide.

Dataset

The model is trained on the Mental Health in Tech Survey dataset from Kaggle, originally collected by OSMI (Open Sourcing Mental Illness).

How the model works

Your 8 answers are converted into numerical values and passed into a trained Random Forest model with 100 decision trees. Each tree votes on an outcome, and the majority vote determines the final prediction.

The model also returns a confidence score and a risk percentage showing the probability of needing treatment.

Input Features Used

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Family history of mental health conditions
  • Frequency of work interference with mental health
  • Remote work status
  • Employer-provided mental health benefits
  • Availability of employer help-seeking resources
  • Anonymity protection when seeking help
